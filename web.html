About Me
Personal Introduction and Background

Hello! I am Chuncheng Liao, also known as Jimmy, a professional specializing in Machine Learning Engineering and Data Science. I hold a Master of Data Science with a specialization in Machine Learning from the University of Sydney.

As part of my master's journey, I interned at FYTO to build a machine learning model that predicts wildfires around the SilverStar Mountain area in Canada. Starting from scratch, Our team gathered data from Google Earth Engine and official sources, then cleaned and engineered features using Python. We built and tested several models, including LightGBM, LSTM, and XGBoost, and deployed the final solution with a ReactJS frontend and a Dockerized backend. This hands-on project taught me how to turn raw data into meaningful predictions and work closely with an industry partner to solve a real-world challenge.

I am passionate about uncovering insights through data and enabling data-driven decision-making. Beyond my technical expertise, I enjoy exploring innovative solutions and thrive in diverse and creative environments.
